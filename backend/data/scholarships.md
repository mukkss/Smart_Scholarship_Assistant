## **1. Post-Matric Scholarship Scheme for Minorities**

**CATEGORY:** Government of India: National Scholarship Portal (NSP) Schemes

**FUNDING BODY:** Ministry of Minority Affairs

**ACADEMIC LEVEL:** Post-Matriculation (Class 11 to PhD)

**BACKGROUND FOCUS:** Students belonging to Minority Communities (Muslim, Christian, Sikh, Buddhist, Parsi, Jain)

### **1. ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:**

- **Academic:** Minimum 50% marks in previous final examination.
- **Income:** Annual family income < **₹2.0 Lakh**.
- **Course:** Class 11–12, UG, PG, ITI, Polytechnic, M.Phil, PhD.
- **Other:** Max **2 students per family** eligible.

### **2. BENEFITS/GRANTS:**

- **Maintenance Allowance:**
    - Hosteller – ₹750/month
    - Day Scholar – ₹330/month
- **Admission & Tuition Fee:** Up to **₹3,000 per annum**
- **Coverage:** 10 months per academic year.

### **3. APPLICATION & DEADLINES:**

- **Portal:** National Scholarship Portal
- **Status:** OPEN (Fresh & Renewal)
- **Tentative Deadline:** **December 15, 2025**
- **Required Documents:** Marksheet, Income Certificate, Minority Certificate, Aadhaar.

## **2. AICTE Pragati Scholarship for Girl Students**

**CATEGORY:** Government of India: NSP Schemes

**FUNDING BODY:** AICTE, Ministry of Education

**ACADEMIC LEVEL:** Technical Degree/Diploma

**BACKGROUND FOCUS:** Girl students in technical education

### **1. ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:**

- Must be admitted to 1st year or 2nd year via lateral entry.
- Studying in **AICTE-approved institution**.
- Annual Family Income < **₹8.0 Lakh**.
- Max **2 girl children per family**.

### **2. BENEFITS/GRANTS:**

- **₹50,000 per annum** (lump sum)
- Covers: fees, books, software, laptops, equipment.

### **3. APPLICATION & DEADLINES:**

- **Portal:** NSP
- **Status:** OPEN
- **Tentative Deadline:** **December 15, 2025**
- **Required Documents:** Marksheet, Admission Proof, Income Certificate, Bank Passbook.

## **4. HDFC Bank Parivartan ECSS Programme**

**CATEGORY:** Private Sector & CSR

**FUNDING BODY:** HDFC Bank Parivartan

**ACADEMIC LEVEL:** Class 1–PG

**BACKGROUND FOCUS:** Students facing financial hardship or crisis

### **1. ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:**

- Minimum **55% marks** (50% for Class 1–6).
- Family income ≤ **₹2.5 Lakh**.
- Preference: crisis cases (death, illness, job loss).

### **2. BENEFITS/GRANTS:**

- Class 1–6 → ₹15,000
- Class 7–12 / ITI / Diploma → ₹18,000
- UG (General) → ₹30,000
- UG (Professional) → ₹50,000
- PG (Professional) → Up to ₹75,000

### **3. APPLICATION & DEADLINES:**

- **Portal:** Buddy4Study
- **Status:** OPEN (Cycle 3)
- **Deadline:** **December 31, 2025**
- **Required Documents:** Marksheet, Income Proof, Crisis Proof, Admission Proof.

## **7. Chevening Scholarship (UK)**

**CATEGORY:** International – Study Abroad

**FUNDING BODY:** Government of the UK

**ACADEMIC LEVEL:** One-year Master’s

**BACKGROUND FOCUS:** Leadership & Networking

### **1. ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:**

- Must hold UG degree equivalent to UK 2:1.
- At least **2,800 hours** of work experience.
- Apply to **3 UK Master’s programs**.
- Must return to home country for 2 years post-scholarship.

### **2. BENEFITS/GRANTS:**

- Fully funded:
    - Full tuition
    - Monthly stipend
    - Travel costs
    - Visa fees
    - Networking events

### **3. APPLICATION & DEADLINES:**

- **Portal:** Chevening Official Website
- **Status:** CLOSED (Reopens Aug 2026)
- **Deadline:** **October 7, 2025**
- **Required Documents:** UG Degree, Work Proof, References, Essays.
